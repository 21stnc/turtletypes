Fun Facts

It is noted for its combative disposition when out of the water with its powerful beak-like jaws, and highly mobile head and neck.

Enviroment

Snapping turtles spend most of their time in the water rather than on land. They are most active dusk when doing their hunting.

Diet

Common snapping turtles are omnivores. They consume both plant and animal matter and are important aquatic scavengers.

Pet Care

The Common Snapping Turtle is an inexpensive, spunky species that, with the right care and husbandry. Please be careful!
