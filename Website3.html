Fun Facts

The gopher tortoise earned its name because it digs deep burrows—like a gopher. Gopher tortoise burrows provide shelter to hundreds of different animals.

Enviroment

This tortoise is found mainly concentrated in Florida. Though it’s most active when the weather is warm, the gopher tortoise spends much of its life inside burrows.
